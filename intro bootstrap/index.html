Announcement

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. It's available on both iOS and Android platforms. The game features two opposing teams of five players each, who must battle to destroy the enemy's base while defending their own. Players select from a diverse roster of heroes, each with unique abilities and playstyles, to form their team composition. The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and skillful execution of tactics such as ganking, laning, and team fights. Mobile Legends has gained a massive player base globally, particularly in Southeast Asia, and has become a prominent title in the mobile gaming industry. It features regular updates, new hero releases, and seasonal events to keep the gameplay fresh and engaging for its community.
The Mobile Legends Professional League (MPL) is a professional esports league for the game Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. It serves as a platform for top players and teams to compete against each other in organized tournaments. The MPL features regional leagues in various countries, with each league having its own set of teams competing for supremacy. These leagues often lead to larger international tournaments where teams from different regions compete against each other for larger prize pools and recognition. The MPL tournaments are highly competitive, with teams showcasing their skills, strategies, and teamwork in intense battles. The league has helped elevate Mobile Legends esports to a professional level, attracting a large audience of fans and spectators both online and at live events. Aside from providing a stage for players to compete, the MPL also contributes to the growth and development of the Mobile Legends esports scene by promoting talent, fostering community engagement, and expanding the game's reach in the competitive gaming landscape.
Mobile Legends follows the classic MOBA gameplay formula where two teams of five players each battle against each other on a map with three lanes and a jungle area. The primary objective is to destroy the enemy's base while protecting your own.
